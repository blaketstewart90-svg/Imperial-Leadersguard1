Organisation Structure

High Command

High Command is the senior leadership of LeadersGuard responsible for directing operations, enforcing discipline, and ensuring the protection of the Imperator.

Middle Command

Mid Command (MIDCOMM) consists of experienced leaders who support High Command by supervising operations, assisting with training, and managing lower ranks.

Low Command

Low Command (LOWCOMM) consists of junior leaders responsible for guiding recruits and guards, maintaining discipline, and assisting in basic operations.
